# irrtracker

**Private investments, clear numbers — on your device.**

irrtracker helps you track private-market and alternative investments (funds, direct deals, real assets, and similar holdings) in one place. You record what you put in, what comes back, and how positions are valued over time. The app turns that into performance measures, charts, and reminders so you can see how each holding and your overall portfolio are doing — without handing your data to a cloud service for analysis.

---

## Who it is for

- Investors who want **money-weighted** performance (returns that respect *when* cash moved) across **multiple projects** and currencies.
- People who already think in terms of **IRR**, **multiples** (DPI, TVPI, RVPI), **NAV**, and **targets** — or who are learning those ideas with in-app explanations.
- Anyone who prefers **local storage**: your portfolio stays on your phone or tablet unless *you* export or share it.

irrtracker does not execute trades, custody assets, or provide personalized investment advice. Strategy-style views (such as **Insights**) are educational snapshots to help you interpret results in context, not recommendations to buy or sell.

---

## What you can do

- **Organize holdings** as *projects* with categories, currencies, and optional targets (for example target IRR or planned hold).
- **Log cash flows** — investments and returns — and **NAV** (valuation) updates, with a clear history over time.
- **See performance** per project and for the whole portfolio: returns, multiples, net capital (cash in minus cash out on a flow basis), and target-based figures where you set a target IRR.
- **Compare and trend**: summary charts, multi-project comparisons, and a sortable projects table.
- **Get reminded** with **alerts** you configure (for example long gaps without a valuation, or a metric crossing a threshold).
- **Work in several currencies** with a single **reference currency** for portfolio-level figures.
- **Export, import, print, and share** — back up data, move between devices, or produce reports for your own records or your advisers.

## Highlights

| Area | What you get |
| ---- | ------------- |
| **Privacy** | Calculations run on your device; you control exports and backups. |
| **Clarity** | Built-in **Help** and metric explanations (including **net capital** vs NAV). |
| **Insights** | Optional view that relates each project to its **category** and **investment strategy** for a quick alignment-style snapshot (prototype feature; not advice). |
| **Reports** | Printable or shareable reports with configurable detail and optional timelines and alert summaries. |
| **Language** | Interface available in several languages; you can follow the system language or pick one in settings. |
| **Valuation date** | Choose **today** or **latest recorded activity** (last cash flow or NAV) as the horizon for metrics — for **both** individual projects **and** portfolio Summary/charts. |
